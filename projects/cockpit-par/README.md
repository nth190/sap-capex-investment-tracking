## Project Overview
This project documents the design and implementation of a **PAR (Investment Request) Management Cockpit**
developed to streamline the end-to-end CAPEX investment workflow.

The cockpit centralizes all PAR-related operations, from early project ideation
to budget control and investment launch, within SAP.

## Business Objectives
- Simplify and secure the CAPEX investment request process
- Provide a single entry point for CAPEX controllers
- Ensure data consistency between investment programs and execution objects
- Enable multi-year budget visibility and control

## Key Functionalities Delivered
### 1. Wishlist Management
- Create future CAPEX projects individually or via mass upload (CSV)
- Pre-budget assignment at early ideation stage
- ALV-based interactive filtering and variant management

### 2. PAR Creation & Validation
- Centralized creation of investment requests
- Automated creation and synchronization of:
  - Investment programs
  - WBS elements or internal orders
  - Assets under construction
- Status-driven workflow from Wishlist to validated investment

### 3. Budget Management
- Initial budget allocation
- Budget re-estimation (increase / decrease)
- Budget reallocation between projects
- Replacement of multiple standard SAP transactions via a single cockpit

### 4. Budget Control & Audit
- Real-time budget consistency checks
- Full traceability of budget movements
- Detailed financial drill-down for CAPEX controllers

## SAP Transactions & Concepts
- SAP IM / PS / FI-AA / CO
- ALV reporting & editable grids
- Mass processing & file upload
- Budget lifecycle management
- End-to-end CAPEX governance
